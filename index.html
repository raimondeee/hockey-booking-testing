About the Coach

Coach Ben Stadey

Coach Photo

Ben Stadey brings a sincere, integrity-driven approach to hockey development — pairing a human-first coaching philosophy with focused, logical skill progressions that players can understand and apply.

Every session is designed to maximize ice time through intentional movement drills, building from skating fundamentals as the base for advanced puck skills including handling, passing, and shooting.

Ben works with players across age groups in the Portland metro area, with sessions held at rinks including Sherwood Ice Arena, Winterhawks Skating Center, and Veterans Memorial Coliseum.

  • Private & small-group on-ice skill development specialist
  • Structured curriculum: skating → puck skills → game application
  • Capped rosters for quality reps and individual attention
  • Portland metro area — multiple rink locations
